1. Description of the Service

RigSize Live is a Progressive Web App (PWA) for the watersports community. The app offers the following features:

  • Real-time sharing of location and gear data (sail size, board volume, kite size) with other users
  • Personal profile with first and last name, gender, date of birth, and nationality for leaderboard functionality
  • Spot Chat — a chat feature per surf spot with support for text and photos
  • Leaderboards and session history
  • Wind forecasts and live weather data from KNMI weather stations
  • Integration with third-party fitness platforms (e.g., Garmin Connect) for automatic activity synchronisation
